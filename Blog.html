CS: GO - Blockbuster transfer market exploded: superstar NiKo officially joined G2 Esports

Nov 01, 2020

This will definitely be one of the biggest contracts in 2020 of professional CS: GO platform.

After nearly 2 months of negotiation, last night, G2 Esports announced that it was officially served by NiKo, a 23-year-old Bosnian superstar. Details of this contract are not disclosed, but according to the announcement of G2 Esports, this is the transfer "most expensive in the history of CS: GO". With the move to G2, NiKo will be playing with his cousin huNter, a dream that has been talked about many times in recent years.
